    About Privacy Requests

    SnapFlow allows you to protect your privacy in event photos with two types of requests:

    Blur Face

    We'll identify your face in all event photos and apply blurring to make you unrecognizable while keeping you in the photos.

    Hide Photos

    Photos containing you will be completely hidden from the event gallery. Only event organizers will be able to see these photos.
