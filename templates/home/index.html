Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about SnapFlow's features, pricing, and how our platform can enhance your event photography experience.

What types of events does SnapFlow support?

SnapFlow supports a wide range of events including personal celebrations (weddings, birthdays, anniversaries), corporate events (conferences, trade shows, company parties), cultural events (festivals, concerts), sports events, academic events, award ceremonies, and public gatherings. Each event type comes with customized features and organization tools.

How does the AI photo tagging work?

Our AI-powered photo tagging uses advanced facial recognition and machine learning algorithms to automatically identify and categorize photos. It can tag photos based on event type, special moments, people, and themes. This significantly reduces the manual effort typically required for photo organization while improving accuracy.

Is SnapFlow secure and private?

Yes, privacy and security are top priorities at SnapFlow. We offer granular privacy controls allowing users to set specific visibility settings for each photo. Our AI-assisted privacy enforcement can automatically blur faces for users who opt out of public sharing. All data is handled in compliance with GDPR and other data protection regulations, with secure encrypted storage.

Can event participants access and download their photos?

Yes, event participants can create a temporary profile to view and download photos they appear in. Event organizers can customize access permissions and download options. We also offer secure watermarking to protect photographers' work from unauthorized use.
